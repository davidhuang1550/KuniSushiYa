Kuni Sushi Ya

Family Owned Japanese Restauant, started by couples whom worked in Japan for over 5 years prior to moving to Canada. With their new found passion of japanese cusine, the couple wanted to share that passion with torontians and thus Kuni Sushi Ya was born.

We put care and love into the quality of our food in order to ensure that each and everyone of our dish prepared to perfection.

Kuni sushi Ya is located in Baldwin Village for over 16 years. You are offered the choice between A-La-Carte or All You Can Eat with your family for friends with a menu including over 100 different dishes. Kuni Sushi Ya provides a large space seating about 150 people.
